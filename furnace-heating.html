SIGNS YOU NEED A FURNACE REPLACEMENT

  • You're calling a technician multiple times a year for repairs.
  • Your energy bills are steadily increasing, even though you're not using your furnace more than usual.
  • You hear banging, scraping, squealing, or rattling sounds from the furnace.
  • A strong burning smell, musty odors, or gas smells from the furnace.
