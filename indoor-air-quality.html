INDOOR AIR QUALITY

Today's homes are sealed tightly for energy efficiency. However, this can sometimes lead to poor ventilation and indoor air quality. Fortunately, at Comfort Pro, we offer air quality audits, duct cleaning, and solutions to improve your home’s air.


SIGNS YOUR HOME DEALS WITH POOR INDOOR AIR QUALITY

  • Frequent allergic reactions or respiratory issues.

    Possible Causes: High levels of dust, mold, pet dander, or chemical pollutants like VOCs (volatile organic compounds).

  • Visible Mold or Mildew Growth.

    Possible Causes: High humidity levels or water damage, which promotes mold growth.

  • Unexplained Headaches or Fatigue.

    Possible Causes: Poor ventilation or exposure to indoor pollutants like carbon monoxide, radon, or VOCs.

HEALTH PROBLEMS

Experiencing these symptoms could signal poor indoor air quality (IAQ) in your home. While they may have other causes, you might feel better when away from home, pointing to IAQ as a likely factor.


  • Irritated eyes, nose, or throat.
  • Coughing or sneezing.
  • Dizziness or lightheadedness.
  • Shortness of breath.
  • Dry or itchy skin.

HOW CAN I STOP INDOOR AIR QUALITY PROBLEMS?

  • 1. Schedule an indoor air quality audit with Comfort Pro. We’ll assess key factors impacting air quality, such as humidity, carbon monoxide, and volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Afterward, we’ll review any issues we find and discuss practical solutions to improve air quality.
  • 2. Minimize the Use of Harsh Chemicals: Cleaning products, air fresheners, and paints can release harmful VOCs, which deteriorate air quality.
  • 3. Regularly Replace HVAC Filters: Dirty filters can clog up your HVAC system and recirculate dust, allergens, and pollutants.
  • 4. Ventilate Your Home: Open windows regularly when weather permits. Use exhaust fans in kitchens and bathrooms to reduce moisture.
  • 5. Use an Air Purifier: Air purifiers with HEPA filter help remove dust, pet danger, pollen, smoke and other allergens.
  • 6. Control Humidity Levels: Use Dehumidifiers in areas with high moisture like basements. Keep humidity levels between 30-50% to prevent mold and dust.
  • 7. Maintain and Clean your HVAC System: Have your HVAC system professionally cleaned and serviced at least once a year with Comfort Pro.
